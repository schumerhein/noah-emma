# **App Name**: Kids Market

## Core Features:

- Product Discovery & Swiping: Allow users to browse product listings through a swipe-based interface, with options to like, buy, or dismiss items.
- Detailed Product View: Display comprehensive product information including description, size, condition, price, seller details, social proof (likes), and buyer protection features.
- Product Search & Filtering: Enable users to search for products by brand, size, or item, and apply advanced filters or browse by category.
- User Messaging & Order Tracking: Facilitate direct communication between buyers and sellers, and provide status updates for ongoing orders (e.g., payment held, item shipped, item received).
- User Profile & Ad Management: Provide users with a personal profile displaying their activity, an 'Impact Dashboard' (savings, items reused), child profiles with growth alerts, and tools to manage their active product advertisements.
- Product Listing & Upload: Guide users through the process of adding new products for sale, including photo upload, categorization, size, condition, and pricing.
- AI-powered Privacy Blurring: Automatically detect and blur faces in uploaded product photos to protect privacy.
- Premium Features & Subscription Management: Offer premium subscriptions to unlock features like unlimited product swipes, advanced search, and an ad-free experience.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A range of soft pinks (e.g., #FFB6C1, #FFB7C5, #F4C2C2) for branding, interactive elements, and accents.
- Background colors: Light theme uses very light pinks/creams (#FFF9FA, #F9F1F2, #FFFFFF), while dark mode uses dark grays/blacks (#1A1617, #111827, #121212, #1A1415).
- Accent colors: Specific colors for status badges (e.g., amber for payment held, blue for shipped, green for received) and a soft green for verified badges.
- Headlines and primary text: 'Manrope' for a modern, approachable feel.
- Body text and general interface text: 'Inter' for high legibility and a clean aesthetic.
- Small, uppercase text elements often use tracking-widest for visual emphasis.
- Use icons from Google Material Design (both 'Material Icons Round' and 'Material Symbols Outlined') for system actions, navigation, and feature representation, favoring a clean, glyph-based style.
- Mobile-first responsive design with a consistent fixed header (for system info or app actions) and a fixed bottom navigation bar across main screens.
- Content areas are typically single-column, with liberal use of padding, rounded containers, and shadows to visually separate elements.
- Subtle transition animations (transition-colors, transition-transform) for interactive elements (buttons, hover states, active states) to provide visual feedback.
- Gentle pulsing (animate-pulse) for indicators like the privacy-blur status.
